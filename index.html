About This Event

Wolverine Security Conference

What is WSC?

The Wolverine Security Conference (WSC) is a volunteer, primarily student run effort by WolvSec - a student organization at the University of Michigan. WSC was born from the Great Lakes Security Conference (GLSC) in collaboration with Michigan Tech University. It was a great success, and we hope to bring the same quality experience and program as last year.

What will you learn at WSC?

The conference is focused on students of all levels, ranging from undergraduate to PhD students, as well as anyone with an interest in security. Because of this focus, the conference focuses on cross-sectioning the entire cybersecurity landscape. The conference hopes to offer further insights into the vast opportunities in cybersecurity.

The WSC CTF Competition

As part of the conference, there will be a CTF. Our esteemed members work tirelessly to bring new and stimulating CTF challenges that we hope can pose varying levels of difficulty and topic.
